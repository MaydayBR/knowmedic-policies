Privacy Policy for Knowmedic

Last updated: August 25, 2025

Knowmedic (“Knowmedic”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) operates the Knowmedic mobile application (the “App”). This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Profile Data: email address and basic profile info (e.g., display name, photo) when you sign up via Firebase Authentication.
  • App Content You Save: items such as folders, bookmarks, and preferences you create in the App.
  • Usage & Device Data: app interactions, diagnostic events, and crash logs (via Firebase services such as Analytics/Crash Reporting if enabled), device model/OS, and a non-permanent device identifier.
  • Support Communications: messages you send to us (e.g., email).

We do not sell personal data. We do not collect your precise location, contacts, or photos.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality (authentication, syncing your saved items).
  • Improve performance, reliability, and security (analytics, debugging, fraud prevention).
  • Personalize in-app experience and surface relevant content.
  • Communicate with you about updates or support.

Data Sources & Third-Party Services

Knowmedic displays public research information retrieved from the PubMed API (National Library of Medicine). PubMed content is not your personal data.

We use trusted vendors to operate the App, including (by example) Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore/Database, Cloud Functions, Analytics/Crash Reporting). These processors handle data on our behalf in accordance with their policies.

Sharing

We share personal data only with service providers that help run the App, and as required by law or to protect rights and safety. We do not sell personal data.

Retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete or anonymize personal data within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain limited information (e.g., security logs or backups) for a short time.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and associated in-app data at any time:

  • In the App: Settings → Delete Account (this triggers removal of your account and your top-level folders/bookmarks stored in our database), or
  • Email us at bmayday20@gmail.com and we’ll assist.
